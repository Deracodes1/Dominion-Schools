Welcome to Dominion Schools

Welcome to the official website of Dominion Schools! We are Catholic mission school located in Abakaliki, the state capital of Ebonyi State, Nigeria. In Dominion Schools, we make it our business to assist every child entrusted to our care reach thier full potentials. Since 2001 when our historic and prestigious school was founded, we have continued to transform lives through an all-round education that prepares young people for the future. Our numerous alumnae scattered in different parts of the world and doing eminently well in their respective professions, bear witness to the lofty standard of education we have achieved over the years. With a current population of about 1200 students drawn from different parts of the country and beyond, Mater possesses a unique diversity that enriches the overall learning experience of the student.

Dr. Veronica Njoku

(Manager)

Our Vision:

To provide an academic community where students are inspired with passion for excellence and are meaningfully engaged towards realizing their potentials with an end result of students’ success, parents satisfaction, staff accomplishment and society’s pride

Moral rectitude, academic excellence, self-confidence, discipline, tolerance, creativity
